Technology Overview

AI Smart Glasses

The company utilizes AI smart glasses that allow customers to capture images, audio, and augmented reality data that overlays real-time digital information onto their environment.

Mobile Testing

Mobile devices support testers by enabling them to pair and control the glasses during field evaluations.

Development Stack

Android/Linux OS, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Unity, and ARCore are used to build AI and AR systems.

Cloud Infrastructure

AWS and Azure handle cloud storage, AI training, and scalable backend systems.

Collaboration Tools

Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace support company-wide documentation and teamwork.

Security Systems

Bitdefender and Norton protect company devices and data under IT supervision.
